The joints in your fingers are the easiest ones to crack,Les articulations de nos doigts sont les plus faciles à craquer, but many people also crack the joints between vertebrae in their neck and back,mais on peut craquer nos articulations entre les vertèbres du cou et du dos, and even their hips, wrists, shoulders and so on.et aussi nos hanches, nos poignets, nos épaules etc. All these joints are synovial joints,Toutes ces articulations sont synoviales and they're the most flexible ones in your body.et sont les plus flexibles de notre corps. The space between the two bones is filled with a viscous liquid,L'espace entre deux os est rempli d'un liquide visqueux, synovial fluid, which contains long, lubricating molecules,le liquide synovial, qui contient de longues molécules lubrifiantes, like hyaluronic acid and lubricin.comme l'acide hyaluronique et la lubricine. When you do that, the bones move away from each other.Quand vous faites cela, les os s'écartent. Inside the joint, the escaping gases form a bubble with a pop.Dans les jointures, les gaz qui s'enfuient forment une bulle et produisent un bruit.
