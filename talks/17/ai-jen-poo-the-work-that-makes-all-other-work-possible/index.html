Ils proposent à la famille de Lily : si elle travaille comme nourrice,They offered Lily's family that if she came to work as their nanny, elle aurait accès à une éducation aux États-Unis,she would be able to have access to a US education, et un salaire hebdomadaire pour les aider.and she would have a weekly salary sent home to help her family financially.
