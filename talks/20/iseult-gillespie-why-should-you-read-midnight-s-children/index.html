Rushdie que, como Saleem, nació en 1947, se educó en la India y en Gran BretañaRushdie, who like Saleem was born in 1947, was educated in India and Britain, y es conocido por sus historias intercontinentales, su comentario políticoand is renowned for his cross-continental histories, political commentary, y su realismo mágico.and magical realism. Para Saleem,And as Saleem sees it, 1001 es "el número de la noche, de la magia, de realidades alternativas".1001 is “the number of night, of magic, of alternative realities.”
