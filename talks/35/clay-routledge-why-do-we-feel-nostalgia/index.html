As Hofer discovered, the cause was not some physical disturbance,Como descubrió Hofer, la causa no era una alteración física, but an intense yearning for their mountain homeland.sino un anhelo intenso por su tierra de montaña. It turned out that anyone separated from their native place for a long timeResultó que cualquier persona separada de su lugar natal durante mucho tiempo was vulnerable to nostalgia.era vulnerable a la nostalgia. And rather than an awful disease,Y más que una terrible enfermedad, it began to be seen as a poignant and pleasant experience.empezó a verse como una experiencia conmovedora y agradable.
