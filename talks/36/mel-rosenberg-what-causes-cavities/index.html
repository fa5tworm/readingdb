The teeth of those ancient humans were riddled with holes.Los dientes de los antiguos humanos estaban plagados de huecos. Their cavities were caused by the same thing that still plagues us today,Sus caries fueron causadas ​​por lo mismo que aún hoy nos aflige, specific tiny microbes that live in our mouths.microbios diminutos específicos que viven en nuestra boca. And as our teeth erupt,Y al aparecer los dientes, they naturally begin to accumulate communities of bacteria.comienzan naturalmente a acumular comunidades de bacterias. Today, an incredible 92% of American adults have had cavities in their teeth.Hoy, un increíble 92 % de los adultos estadounidenses han tenido caries.
