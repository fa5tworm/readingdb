This is similar to our own brains,Es similar a nuestro propio cerebro, which learn from previous experiences to shape how new stimuli are processed.que aprende de experiencias anteriores para procesar los nuevos estímulos. Recognition algorithms aren't just limited to facial expressions.Los algoritmos de reconocimiento no se limitan solo a las expresiones faciales.
